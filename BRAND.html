06 — Voice

Tone of Voice

Supellex speaks like a confident, knowledgeable brand — not a salesperson. Every word should earn its place.

Do

  • Short, punchy sentences
  • Benefit-first copy
  • British spelling always
  • Formal but warm in policies
  • Let the product speak

Don't

  • Exclamation marks
  • Vague filler ("amazing quality")
  • American spelling
  • Over-use of "We" / "Our"
  • Emoji in formal copy
Instead ofWrite
Great value for moneyPremium quality. Fair price. No showroom markup.
Amazing wardrobe!Our best-spec wardrobe — zero compromise.
We offer free shippingFree UK delivery on every order.
customizecustomise
