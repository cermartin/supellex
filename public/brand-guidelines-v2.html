06

Tone of Voice

Supellex speaks like a confident, knowledgeable brand — not a salesperson. Every word should earn its place.

Do

  • Short, punchy sentences
  • Lead with the benefit, not the feature
  • British spelling — always
  • Formal but warm in policy copy
  • Let the product speak for itself

Don't

  • Exclamation marks
  • Vague filler — "amazing quality", "great value"
  • American spelling — customize, color, etc.
  • Overuse of "We" and "Our"
  • Emoji in formal or product copy

Examples

Instead ofWrite
Great value for money!Premium quality. Fair price. No showroom markup.
Amazing wardrobeOur best-spec wardrobe — zero compromise.
We offer free shippingFree UK delivery on every order.
customize / colorcustomise / colour
